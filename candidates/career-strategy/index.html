CAREER STRATEGY

Build a luxury hospitality career that stands out.

Expert advice, actionable tips, and proven strategies to help you advance your career in Nigeria's luxury hospitality sector.

Your career deserves precision too.

At AfriLux Hospitality, we don't just place talent – we help you build a career that matches your ambition. Whether you're a rising star or an experienced leader, the right strategy can accelerate your path to executive roles in luxury hotels, resorts, and restaurants across Africa.

Here's our guide to standing out, advancing faster, and securing the opportunities you deserve.

4 Pillars of Career Success

Master these areas to accelerate your path to leadership.

CV & Personal Branding

Your CV is your first impression. Make it count.

  • Tailor every CV to the specific role
  • Quantify achievements (e.g., "Increased revenue by 25%")
  • Use industry keywords (Fine Dining, Revenue Management)
  • Keep it concise – 2 pages max for senior roles

Interview Mastery

Prepare to impress at every stage of the interview process.

  • Research the property deeply
  • Prepare STAR examples for behavioural questions
  • Ask insightful questions about operations
  • Follow up within 24 hours with a thank-you

Networking & Relationships

Your network is your net worth in hospitality.

  • Connect with industry leaders on LinkedIn
  • Attend hospitality events and conferences
  • Join professional associations (e.g., HFTP, HOSPA)
  • Build genuine, long-term relationships

Continuous Learning

Stay ahead with ongoing development.

  • Pursue certifications (CCHM, AHLEI)
  • Attend industry workshops and webinars
  • Read hospitality thought leaders
  • Seek feedback and mentorship
