CONFIDENTIALITY

Your privacy is our priority.

At AfriLux Hospitality, we understand that discretion is essential in executive search. We protect your data, your identity, and your career ambitions with the highest level of confidentiality.

Trust is the foundation of everything we do.

Whether you're a candidate exploring new opportunities or an employer seeking elite talent, we treat your information with absolute discretion. Our confidentiality framework ensures that your personal data, search activity, and career ambitions remain private – unless and until you choose to share them.

We operate with the same level of discretion you'd expect from a world-class luxury hospitality partner. Because for us, trust isn't just a value – it's a guarantee.

Our Confidentiality Framework

Four pillars that protect your privacy at every stage.

Ironclad NDAs

Every engagement is protected by a signed Non-Disclosure Agreement. Your information is never shared without explicit consent.

Identity Protection

Candidate identities are anonymised until they approve being presented to a client. You control when and how you're introduced.

Secure Data Storage

All personal data is stored securely and accessed only by authorised team members. We comply with GDPR and Nigerian data protection standards.

Right to Erasure

You can request to have your profile and personal data deleted from our systems at any time – no questions asked.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our confidentiality practices.

How do you protect my personal information?
We use industry-standard encryption to store all personal data. Access is restricted to authorised team members only. We also comply with GDPR and Nigerian data protection regulations, and we sign NDAs with all clients and candidates.
Will my current employer find out I'm looking?
No. We never share your identity or search activity with any third party without your explicit permission. We understand the sensitivity of executive job searches and operate with complete discretion.
How do you handle candidate introductions?
We only present candidate profiles to employers after receiving your explicit consent. You control when and how you're introduced, and we always anonymise profiles until you approve the introduction.
What happens if I want my data deleted?
You can request to have your profile and personal data permanently deleted from our systems at any time. Simply email us at acquire@afriluxhospitality.com with your request.
Do you share my data with third parties?
We never sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is only shared with potential employers as part of a confidential search, and only with your explicit consent.
How long do you keep my data?
We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can request deletion at any time, and we will remove your data within 30 days.

Have questions about privacy?

We're here to help. If you have any questions or concerns about our confidentiality practices, please don't hesitate to reach out.
