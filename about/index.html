Our Journey

From a bold idea to Nigeria's trusted hospitality talent partner.

2020
FOUNDATION

Genesis

Founded by hospitality veterans who identified the critical gap between Nigeria's luxury properties and elite talent. Started with executive search for Lagos' premier hotels.

2021
EXPANSION

National Footprint

Expanded to Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Calabar. Launched our proprietary Precision Search methodology and built Nigeria's first elite hospitality talent database.

2022
INNOVATION

Process Standardization

Introduced the 72-Hour Guarantee for urgent leadership positions and formalized our rigorous vetting process for all candidates.

2023
TECH INTEGRATION

Launched our digital platform combining job board functionality with elite recruitment services, making talent access seamless for employers and candidates.

LEADERSHIP

Market Leadership

Became the trusted partner for leading hospitality groups across Nigeria, recognized for delivering candidates who drive measurable business growth.
