Our Journey

From a bold idea to Nigeria's trusted hospitality talent partner.

2020
FOUNDATION

Genesis

Founded by hospitality veterans who identified the critical gap between Nigeria's luxury properties and elite talent. Started with executive search for Lagos' premier hotels.

2021
EXPANSION

National Footprint

Expanded to Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Calabar. Launched our proprietary Precision Search methodology and built Nigeria's first elite hospitality talent database.
